About the Doctor

Dr. Sweety Sharma

MBBS · MS (ENT) — Ear, Nose & Throat Specialist

Dr. Sweety Sharma is a qualified ENT Specialist (MBBS, MS) based in Betul, Madhya Pradesh. With a patient-first approach and advanced clinical training, she provides comprehensive ear, nose and throat care for patients of all ages — from newborns to the elderly.

Practising at Khusi Medical on Link Road, Betul, Dr. Sharma is known for her warm, attentive consultations and effective treatments. She is fluent in Hindi and dedicated to making ENT care accessible to every family in and around Betul.

Our Services

Comprehensive ENT Treatment in Betul

Expert care for all ear, nose and throat conditions using modern diagnostic equipment and proven treatments.

👂

Ear Care

Diagnosis and treatment of all ear disorders, from common infections to hearing loss and balance issues.

  • Hearing Loss & Tinnitus
  • Ear Infections (Otitis)
  • Eardrum Perforation
  • Vertigo & Dizziness
  • Ear Wax Removal
  • Paediatric Ear Problems
👃

Nose & Sinus

Complete nasal and sinus care including allergy management and surgical interventions where needed.

  • Chronic Sinusitis
  • Nasal Polyps
  • Deviated Nasal Septum
  • Allergic Rhinitis
  • Nasal Obstruction
  • Nose Bleeds (Epistaxis)
🗣️

Throat & Voice

Full throat assessment from tonsillitis and voice disorders to snoring and cancer screening.

  • Tonsillitis & Tonsillectomy
  • Adenoid Problems
  • Voice Hoarseness
  • Snoring & Sleep Apnoea
  • Throat Infections
  • Throat Cancer Screening
